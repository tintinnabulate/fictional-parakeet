There will be a charge for transport. Description below. If you are interested please e-mail/message John via the Facebook EURYPAA2016 page

"The west country, including Bath and Bristol are ancient lands belonging to the old tribal peoples that inhabited the land of Albion (as England was then known): they were called the Dobunni. Within these lands is a wealth of history and ancient majick - from the sacred isle of Avalon (Glastonbury) with its incredible Tor Piercing the heavens, joining heaven and earth, to the breathtaking Chalice well gardens and white spring, where the sacred red and white waters converge and pour from the earth yards apart. Waters renowned throughout the world for there healing and rejuvenating properties, Glastonbury hosts the only Temple in the UK totally dedicated to the Goddess and of course the shops with every form of esoteric book and Chrystal known to mankind (also very good food and coffee). We will complete this Ancient land experience by going to Stanton drew stone circle in the valley of Chew, one of the country's oldest standing stone circles (3 altogether) to complete our Magical Day out. Join us on a day to remember forever."

Bill the Druid
