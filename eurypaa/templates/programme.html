Al-Anon/Alateen

There are big plans for Al-Anon at Bath. For the first time at Eurypaa we are planning a full programme of Al-Anon events, which will happen in a room dedicated to Al-Anon and Alateen. In addition to a few speaker meetings we plan two workshops on Al-Anon topics. More on this soon. There will also be a reception meeting where we will greet all those interested and/or curious about Al-Anon. We will have leaflets available. There will be an Al-Anon literature stand with lots of books and leaflets for sale. We have not finalised it yet, but if feasible we will have an Alateen meeting. At the conventionâ€™s main speaker meetings we will have an Al-Anon speaker and all being well and Alateen speaker too. So if your primary fellowship is Al-Anon there will be lots going on to serve you. If you are a duel member (or double winner as we like to call you) or a member of AA alone, bring your Al-Anon family and friends to the convention knowing they will not be left out.
